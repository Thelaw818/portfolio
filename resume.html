Pigeon Racing in Southern California

My Pigeon Racing Experience

The Fernando Valley Pigeon Club is a dedicated group of pigeon enthusiasts in the Fernando Valley area. We are passionate about the sport of pigeon racing, fostering camaraderie among members, and promoting the health and well-being of our racing birds. We organize races, provide support for our members, and participate in local and regional competitions. Our club is committed to upholding the highest standards of ethical racing practices and responsible pigeon ownership....

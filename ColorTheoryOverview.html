Color Theory Overview

What is Color Theory?

Color theory is a set of guidelines that describe the relationships between colors. It's a crucial aspect of art, design, and other visual fields, helping artists and designers create harmonious and impactful color palettes. Understanding color theory allows for the effective communication of ideas and emotions through visual design.
